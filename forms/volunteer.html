This volunteer agreement (Agreement) is entered into as of the date set forth below by Saving Orphan Souls Rescue. WHEREAS, the Volunteer has voluntarily contacted Saving Orphan Souls Rescue and has expressed an interest in working with cats/animals in the care and custody of Saving Orphan Souls Rescue/Petco. In consideration of Saving Orphan Souls Rescue agreement to allow the volunteer to view and/or interact with such animals, the volunteer hereby, for his/her heirs, his/her personal representatives, and him/herself represent and warrant as follows: 1. The volunteer is fully aware of the risks that cats pose and have elected to view and/or interact with one or more cats in the care of Saving Orphan Souls Rescue/Petco voluntarily. The volunteer knowingly assumes all risks that exposure to cats may pose, including but not limited to bodily injury and/or death. 2. THE VOLUNTEER HEREBY WAIVES, RELEASES DISCHARGES, HOLD HARMLESS, AND PROMISES TO INDEMNIFY AND NOT TO BRING SUIT AGAINT SAVING ORPHAN SOULS RESCUE, its directors, officers, volunteers, staff, and all other agents, and attorney for any of the referenced parties and any other parties acting for or on behalf of the referenced partied from an and all rights and claims which the volunteer has or which may hereafter accrue to the volunteer and from any and all damages which may be sustained by the volunteer directly or indirectly in connection with, or arising out of, the volunteers exposure to such animals.
